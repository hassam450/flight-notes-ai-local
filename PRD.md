# **Product Requirements Document: Flight Notes AI**

## **1\. Project Overview**

**Flight Notes AI** is an all-in-one study and note management application specifically designed for student pilots. The platform leverages AI to provide summarization, transcription, and flashcard generation to streamline the aviation learning process. The goal is to deliver a clean, reliable, and scalable MVP.

## **2\. Target Platforms**

* **iOS**  
* **Android**  
* *Recommended Framework: React Native (for cross-platform efficiency).*

## **3\. Core Functional Requirements**

### **3.1 Onboarding & Authentication**

* **Splash Screen:** Branded welcome/loading experience.  
* **Sign-in Options:** Google, Apple, and Email (optional).  
* **Profile Management:** User profile setup and secure session handling.

### **3.2 Recording & Upload**

* **Audio Recorder:** In-app tool for capturing flight notes or lectures.  
* **File Picker:** Support for uploading existing audio files and text documents.  
* **Visual Feedback:** Upload progress and processing/status indicators.  
* **Categorization:** Assigning notes to user-selected categories (e.g., PPL, Instrument, Commercial).

### **3.3 AI Integration**

* **Transcription:** Automatic conversion of audio recordings to text.  
* **Summarization:** AI-generated summaries of lengthy notes or recordings.  
* **Flashcards:** Automated generation of study flashcards from note content.  
* **Comparison View:** Ability to toggle between original content and AI-generated outputs.

### **3.4 Test Yourself (Assessment)**

* **MCQs:** Topic-based multiple-choice quizzes.  
* **Oral Exam Practice:** AI-driven mock exams with text or optional voice-based interactions and basic scoring feedback.

### **3.5 Aviation Toolkit**

* **AI Chatbot:** An aviation-specific assistant for quick queries.  
* **Resource Library:** Centralized access to aviation materials.  
* **FAA PDF Viewer:** In-app viewer for official FAA documents and manuals.

### **3.6 Account & History**

* **Note History:** Accessible log of all past recordings and notes.  
* **Session Details:** Review of specific saved study sessions.  
* **User Settings:** Customization and account management.

## **4\. Monetization**

* **Paywall:** Dedicated screen for subscription plans.  
* **Subscription Logic:** Integration for recurring payments.  
* **Management:** "Restore Purchases" functionality and support for free trials.

## **5\. UI/UX Guidelines**

* **Theme:** Modern, sleek design with a primary **Purple-themed** color palette.  
* **Typography:** Clean, legible fonts suitable for educational content.  
* **Navigation:** Intuitive and mobile-first usability.

## **6\. Technical & Development Expectations**

* **Ownership:** Drew (Client) retains 100% of source code and IP.  
* **Source Control:** Mandatory GitHub/GitLab access from day one with transparent commit history.  
* **Maintenance:** Minimum 30-day post-launch bug fix window.  
* **Deployment:** Developer assistance with Apple App Store and Google Play Store submissions.

## **7\. Development Roadmap**

### **Phase 1 (MVP)**

* Authentication & User Profiles.  
* Recording, Uploading, and Categorization.  
* AI Core: Transcription, Summaries, and Flashcards.  
* "Test Yourself" module (MCQs & Oral Practice).  
* Aviation Toolkit & AI Chatbot.  
* Resource Library & PDF Integration.

## **8\. Success Criteria**

* Seamless upload and organization of notes.  
* High accuracy in audio transcription and AI-generated study aids.  
* Stable application performance with proper error handling.  
* Successful deployment to both major app stores.
